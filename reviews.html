Pan's Bake Shop

Pan's bakeshop. We tasted chocolate, vanilla and mint choc chip. Starting from the top, the ice-cream itself was quite flavourful and the texture particularly defined. However, as you work your way down, you make it to the cone. Integrety wise, the cone has a very good structure. We were provided with a waffle cone, and given the option of a tub. Unfortunately, Pan's lacked more creative cones, such as chocolate waffle or traditional waffle. In addition, the flavour of the cone was somewhat lackluster, however, the fact that we visited in the ice of January meant that the flavour may have been compromised due to the lack of customers and extended shelf time. The price per scoop was generous, at 1.50 GBP per scoop. Overall, a pleasant experience.
