Pan's Bake Shop

As far as ice-cream goes, Pan's Bake Shop provides some good ice-cream flavours. Between the three of us, we tried: chocolate, vanilla and mint choc chip. Starting from the top, the ice-cream itself was quite flavourful and the texture was close to impeccable. However, as you work your way down, you make it to the cone. Although the cone had good integrety, the flavour of the cone was somewhat lackluster. The fact that we visited in the cold of January meant that the flavour may have been compromised due to the lack of customers and extended shelf time.
